# Founder Weekly Reflection

**Cadence:** Once per week. Takes 15–20 minutes. The only rule is honesty.

This artifact applies the SAGA structure to the founder's own experience — not just the system's metrics. It is a governing document for the person building the thing.

---

## How to Use This

Each week, copy the template below into a new dated entry under **Reflection Entries**. Fill it in. Do not optimize for how it sounds. The audience is you, one year from now.

---
