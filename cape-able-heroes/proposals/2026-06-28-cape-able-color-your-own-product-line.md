## 1. Current State Assessment

Cape-Able Heroes has a validated first field signal around tangible, hands-on identity artifacts from the Aviation WonderLab. The current offer ladder includes a Cape-Able Hero Kit, but the exact entry-level physical product family has not been defined or tested with buyers.

New concept assets now show a coherent Color Your Own product family:

- Color Your Own Cape
- Color Your Own Stickers
- Color Your Own Magnets

These assets suggest a strong product direction, but visual strength is not the same as market validation.

## 2. Target State Definition

The product line should move forward only if real buyers show demand. A validated target state would include:

- A clearly preferred first product variant
- Parent or host willingness to buy
- Evidence that children can personalize the product without being rescued
- Language that frames the product as evidence of capability, not a confidence promise
