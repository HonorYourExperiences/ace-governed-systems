# Cape-Able Heroes – Decision Log (v1)

**Purpose:** Lightweight, permanent record of judgment calls made during Cape-Able Heroes development.  
**Rule:** One to three sentences per decision. Log it the day it is made.  
**Status:** Active.

Decisions that are not written down disappear. The reasoning that built this thing is part of the product. Capture it.

---

## What to Log

Log any decision where you chose one path over another and the reason matters. Examples:
- Pricing choices
- Who to contact first and why
- What to include or cut from a session
- Which channel to try first
- How to respond to an objection
- What to defer and why

You do not need to log routine task completion. Log decisions.

---

## Decision Entry Template

```
---
**Date:** 
**Decision:** [What did you decide?]
**Why:** [The actual reason — not the polished reason. What made you choose this?]
**Alternatives considered:** [What else was on the table?]
**How to revisit:** [What signal would tell you this decision was wrong?]
---
```
