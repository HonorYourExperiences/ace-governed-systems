## What This Is

Cape-Able Color Your Own is a tangible product line that lets children turn creative play into a wearable or displayable identity artifact.

The current concept family includes:

- Color Your Own Cape
- Color Your Own Stickers
- Color Your Own Magnets

These are not positioned as generic crafts. They are Cape-Able identity objects: children color, personalize, display, wear, or place the thing they made, then carry visible evidence of their own expression.

## Current Governance Position

This product family may become the entry-level physical product line for Cape-Able Heroes, but it should not be treated as proven until buyer demand is tested.

Initial recommended validation path:

1. Use as a post-WonderLab continuation offer.
2. Offer to parents or hosts after a real session.
3. Track expressed interest, preorders, or paid purchases.
4. Compare cape vs sticker vs magnet demand before manufacturing.
