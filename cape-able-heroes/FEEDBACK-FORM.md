# Cape-Able Heroes – Post-Session Feedback Forms (v1)

**Purpose:** Capture real evidence from parents and facilitators after each WonderLab session.  
**How to use:** Print and hand out at close of session, or read aloud and fill in verbally. Collect before families leave.  
**Status:** v1 — ready for pilot sessions.
