## Objective

Transition Cape-Able Heroes from a validated pilot offer into active revenue generation and early market scaling — reaching paying customers across multiple channels and gathering the testimonials and case studies needed to sustain growth.

---

## Current State (End of Phase 2 → Beginning of Phase 3)

- WonderLab session format is documented, tested, and refined through Phase 1 pilot sessions.
- Take-Home Hero Kit, Educator/Facilitator Toolkit, pricing tiers, and delivery standards are finalized (Phase 2).
- Field evidence, parent feedback, and at least one paid session exist from Phase 1 validation.
- Outreach templates (warm leads, schools, camps, libraries, churches) are ready from Phase 1.

- No multi-channel testing has been completed to identify which buyer types and channels convert best.

- Active revenue from multiple paid WonderLab activations.

## Gap-Closing Actions

### Action 1 — Begin Systematic Institutional Outreach
Use the Phase 1 outreach templates (OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md) as the base. Add a formal outreach log (CHANNEL-TESTING-TRACKER.md) to track contacts, responses, and conversions across all channels:
- Schools and after-school programs
- Camps and youth programs
- Libraries and community organizations
- Churches and faith-based family programs
- Direct parent groups and neighborhood networks

### Action 2 — Run Paid Activations
Deliver WonderLab sessions to paying customers using the documented session format (SESSION-FORMAT.md). Each activation is also a data collection event. Use the PAID-ACTIVATION-PLAYBOOK.md for logistics and preparation.

### Action 3 — Capture Testimonials and Case Studies
After each paid session, collect formal testimonials and build at least one complete case study. Use TESTIMONIAL-CASE-STUDY.md as the template and collection tool.

### Action 4 — Test Channels and Analyze Conversion
Track which outreach channels, buyer types, and price points convert. After 5–10 outreach contacts per channel, run a SAGA Analyze cycle using the CHANNEL-TESTING-TRACKER.md data. Identify the highest-converting path and prioritize it.
