# Cape-Able Color Your Own - Sourcing Resources

**Purpose:** Practical resource map for prototyping and validating the Color Your Own cape, sticker, and magnet product family.
**Status:** Research snapshot, 2026-06-28. Verify price, MOQ, lead time, and compliance before purchasing.

## Recommended Build Path

### Path A - Fast Prototype

Use this for the next WonderLab or parent/host interest test.

| Item | Resource Type | Why It Fits |
|---|---|---|
| Blank canvas capes | Off-the-shelf DIY cape packs | Fastest way to test child engagement before custom manufacturing |
| Bulk fabric markers | Classroom craft supply | Low-cost group use; prioritize non-toxic and ASTM D-4236 labeling |
| Printable vinyl sticker sheets | Local print/cut prototype | Lets us test sticker demand before die-cut production |
| Printable magnet sheets | Local print/cut prototype | Lets us test magnet display/remix behavior at home |

### Path B - Short-Run Product Test

Use this after parents/hosts show actual purchase interest.

| Item | Resource Type | Why It Fits |
|---|---|---|
| Custom printed fabric | Print-on-demand fabric or low-MOQ fabric printer | Lets us test repeat line-art cape fabric before production inventory |
| Custom die-cut stickers | Short-run sticker printer | Produces cleaner buyer-facing sticker packs |
| Custom magnets | Short-run magnet printer | Produces buyer-facing keepsake/display products |

### Path C - Manufacturing Quote

Use this only after validation targets are met:

- 5+ written parent/host yes responses, or
- 3+ paid preorders, or
- 1+ host asks for group pack pricing.

Request quotes for:

- Printed/sewn kids cape with safety closure
- Printed sticker sheet, kiss-cut or die-cut
- Flexible magnet sheet or individual die-cut magnets
- ASTM/CPSIA documentation
- Packaging and warning labels

## First Purchase List

For a low-cost validation kit:

1. One pack of blank DIY capes.
2. One bulk pack of fabric markers with ASTM D-4236 / non-toxic labeling.
3. One pack of printable vinyl sticker sheets.
4. One pack of printable magnetic sheets.
5. Clear resealable bags or envelopes for prototype kit packaging.
6. Simple one-question parent/host interest card:
   - "Which would you actually buy or add to a session: cape, stickers, or magnets?"
   - "Would you prefer one child kit or a group pack?"

## Quote Questions For Vendors

Ask each vendor:

1. What is the minimum order quantity?
2. What is the unit cost at 25, 50, 100, and 250 units?
3. What is the lead time?
4. Can they provide CPSIA / ASTM / material safety documentation?
5. Are markers non-toxic and ASTM D-4236 labeled?
6. For magnets: do they meet relevant magnet and small-parts safety requirements for children?
7. For capes: what closure is used, and is it safe for children?
8. Can they provide a sample before bulk production?

## Current Recommendation

Start with **Path A - Fast Prototype**.

Do not quote custom manufacturing until the next parent/host follow-up confirms which product variant people would actually buy.
