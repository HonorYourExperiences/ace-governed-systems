# Cape-Able Color Your Own - Risk Review

**Purpose:** Prevent this product line from drifting into craft-only, aspiration-theater, or unsupported outcome claims.

## FMEA-Style Risk Table

| Failure Mode | Effect | Risk Level | Control |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product becomes generic craft merchandise | Weakens Cape-Able identity architecture | High | Tie every product to a hero name, strength, mission, or reflection prompt |
| Marketing claims confidence without evidence | Violates Evidence & Capability | High | Use evidence-first language: "children personalize and carry/display what they made" |
| Cape is framed as magic transformation | Creates aspiration theater | Medium | Avoid "become confident instantly" language |
| Parent sees it as clutter | Reduces purchase intent | Medium | Magnets/stickers should have home/classroom display use |
| Production cost is too high | Poor margin or delayed launch | Medium | Validate with printable/local prototype before manufacturing |
| Child needs adult rescue to complete it | Undermines Way Through | Medium | Designs must be simple enough for independent personalization |
| Safety/material issue | Buyer trust risk | High | Require age-appropriate materials, non-toxic markers, choking-hazard review, and washable/permanent labeling |

## Required Language Guardrails

Use:

- "color, personalize, and wear/display/place"
- "evidence of what they created"
- "low-barrier creative entry point"
- "continuation of the WonderLab identity moment"

Avoid:

- "gives children confidence"
- "transforms kids instantly"
- "makes every child brave"
- "guaranteed self-esteem"

## Axiomatic Fit

| Axiom | Fit |
|---|---|
| Inherent Sufficiency | Product starts from "Be You," not "earn worth" |
| Way Through | Child creates and personalizes the artifact |
| Witnessing & Mature Presence | Parent/host can witness what the child chose and made |
| Evidence & Capability | Artifact is visible evidence, not an unsupported claim |
| Audit Integrity | Product must stay tied to validation notes and buyer signals |

## Go / No-Go Gate

Move forward only when there is buyer evidence. The current visuals are strong concept evidence, not market validation.
